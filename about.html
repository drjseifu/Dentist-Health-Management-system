About Dembia Primary Hospital


Dembia primary hospital is founded in 1998, located in Amhara region, Dembia Woreda, Kolladiba Kebele.
the hospital helps the society to assist in reducing the number of vulnerable people through improving access to health facilities, fighting corona virus, HIV/AIDS, malaria, T.B and other diseases, in developing workable resources generation mechanismso as to become a self-reliant national society.
